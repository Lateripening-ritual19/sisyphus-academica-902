sisyphus-academica-902 helps researchers manage their academic workflow. This tool automates literature reviews, generates new research ideas, checks citations, and reviews papers. It uses machine learning to assist with tasks that usually take weeks. Use this software to save time and improve the quality of your academic output.

## 🛠 Features

This application simplifies the research cycle. 

- Literature Review: Scan thousands of arXiv papers to find relevant data for your specific topic.
- Novelty Generation: Find gaps in current research to suggest new topics for your studies.
- Citation Verification: Confirm that every reference in your document exists and matches the source.
- Adversarial Review: Simulate a peer review process to find weaknesses in your writing before you submit it.
- Latex Integration: Export your work directly into industry-standard formatting for publishing.

## 🔍 How to Perform a Literature Review

1. Launch the application from your desktop icon.
2. Select the "Literature Review" tab from the main menu.
3. Enter your research keywords into the search bar.
4. Set the date range to define how far back the software should look for papers.
5. Click the "Scan" button. 
6. The software presents a list of relevant papers. You can mark these as read or save them to your local project folder.

## 📝 Generating New Research Ideas

The novelty generation tool compares your current notes against a database of published work. 

1. Go to the "Novelty" tab.
2. Upload your existing research notes or your current paper draft.
3. Click "Analyze."
4. The system produces a list of potential research gaps. It explains why these gaps exist based on the literature it found during the scan.

## 🛡 Verifying Citations

Citation mistakes hurt your reputation. Use this tool to prevent errors.

1. Open the "Citation" tab.
2. Import your document by clicking "Browse" and selecting your file.
3. The software compares your references against global databases.
4. Any broken or mismatched citations appear in red text.
5. Click on an error to see the suggested correction.

## 🏗 Understanding the Research Pipeline

The software uses a multi-agent system. This means different parts of the program talk to each other to solve problems. One agent scans the internet for papers. A second agent formats the data. A third agent checks the logic of your arguments. This approach mimics a collaborative research team.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What if the application freezes during a scan?
This can happen if you search for too many terms at once. Close the application and restart it. The software uses a cache so your work remains saved.

Can I use this for non-academic writing?
Yes, the tools work for any formal writing that requires citations and logical structure.

How does the software access the internet?
It uses standard web requests to reach arXiv and other research databases. It does not track your personal browsing history.

Is my data private?
Your research data lives on your computer. You control your files at all times. The software does not send your drafts to a central server unless you ask it to perform a specific cloud-based check.
